The Audacity Sequence

Four steps. Three focused hours. One move you won't undo.

Every session runs the same proprietary sequence. Built across 200+ live rooms. Refined until it works on the exact person who's been "getting ready" for years.

  • 01 The Room. We go live. You, and a small group of people who raised their hand for the exact same reason you did. No lurking. No cameras off.
  • 02 The Naming. We isolate the one move you've been avoiding. Not ten moves. Not a life plan. The specific thing your 3am brain already knows you need to do.
  • 03 The Crossing. You make it. Live. While the room is watching and doing theirs. The timer is running. Your old escape routes are closed. This is what you came for.
  • 04 The Anchor. Before you close the tab, we lock in what just happened so it can't quietly un-happen by Thursday. You leave different. On purpose.

Questions You Probably Have

The short answers.

What is The Audacity Sequence, exactly? +
Four-step live group process — Room, Naming, Crossing, Anchor. It converts knowledge you already have into a specific move you make before the session ends. We won't spoil the mechanics inside each step. That's part of what makes it work.
Do I need to prepare anything? +
No. Don't read up. Don't mentally rehearse. The less prepared you are, the better this works. Just clear three focused hours and show up on time.
Can I just watch the replay? +
No replay is sent. The Sequence only works live — the mechanism depends on the room, the timer, and the move happening in real time. This is by design, not marketing.
What if I'm nervous? +
Good. If you weren't, you wouldn't need this. Nervous means you're exactly the kind of person this was built for.
Is it really free? +
Yes. No upsell during the session. No "course" waiting at the end. The live bonuses go to people who show up. That's it.
What if I can't do The Crossing on the day? +
You can always pass. No pressure, no judgement. But in our experience — once the group starts moving, you'll want in.
