Something just changed · quietly · permanently

The era that made you valuable just ended.

For thirty years we lived in the Knowledge Age. The rules were simple. The people who knew more, won more. Best-read, best-educated, best-informed — they ran the table. Knowledge was the asset. Knowledge was the moat. Knowledge was the edge.

Then, overnight, AI retired an entire category of human value.

Whatever you spent ten years learning, anyone with a phone can pull in ten seconds. The advantage you built your career on just evaporated. So did everyone else's. Knowledge went from rare to free in the time it took to update an app.
